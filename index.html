Tom's Overwatch Showcase

Tanks

The role of the tank in Overwatch is simple. Each team has a single tank and their job is to stay at the very front of their team and leverage their high health and powerful attacks to dominate the enemy team. Due to the tank's vital importance, their actions can ulimately sway the outcome of a match

I play the following 4 tank heroes:

Reinhardt

Reinhardt has a giant hammer that he swings at any enemy in his vicinity, and an energy barrier that protects the team from projectiles. This hammer can launch a fireball once every 6 seconds, holding a maximum of 2 at once. Reinhardt can also charge at enemies, pinning them and often crashing them into a wall

D.Va

D.va pilots a mech-suit that has both offensive and defensive features. This includes automatic guns with infinite ammo and a flurry of missiles that can be fired every 8 seconds. Additionally, the mech has a defense-matrix which protrudes from the front and vaporises any projectiles that enter it

Ramattra

Ramattra posesses a sceptre/ staff containing 100 small pellets that it shoots extremely fast. He also has an alternate form (Nemesis Form) where he grows larger and gains an extre set of arms, allowing him to punch enemies or block projectiles. Furthermore, he has an orb which when thrown, casts a strong gravity-field that slows enemies and damages them slighty

Doomfist

Doomfist has a robotic arm, a knuckle-integrated rechargable shotgun and he is the fastest-moving tank. His shotgun has 4 in the chamber, and he can punch with his robotic arm every 4 seconds. He can also block projectiles with his arm (which charges it) and slam enemies which gives him a slight health boost

Damage

Damage heroes are the main attackers of their team, thus the name. Their main objective is to kill all opposing players by utlilsing their movement and attacking abilities. There are 2 damage heroes per team, however in a standard match, both players cannot play the same hero at the same time

I play the following 5 damage heroes:

Genji

Genji has shurikens which he throws in threes. There are 2 ways of throwing them. Either he fires all 3 in a straight trajactory, or in a more spread-out fashion (for close-range combat). Also, his sword can deflect any incoming projectile and he can dash forward every 8 seconds, or after an elimination

Ashe

Ashe posesses a 2-in-1 shotgun-sniper with 12 bullets in the chamber. She also has a sawn-off shotgun which when fired, blows herself in the opposite directon and any impacted target forwards (away from her). Additionally, Ashe has a dynamite grenade which detonates 5 seconds after landing, or immediately after she shoots it

Tracer

Tracer dual-wields automatic pistols, and is one of the best damage heroes due to her mobility. She can 'blink' a few meters in any direction around her (these recharge every 3 seconds) holding a maximum of 3 blinks at once. She can also 'recall' which returns her back to the location she was at with the highest health in the past 3 seconds

Hanzo

Hanzo is Genji's brother, and his weapon of choice is a bow with a variety of different arrows. As well as normal arrows, the bow has a tracker-arrow which reveals all enemies who enter its spherical radius once it lands. He also has a 'rapid-arrows' ability, which fires 5 consecutive arrows very quickly

Reaper

Reaper's weapons of choice are two oversized shotguns which deal very high damage at close range. He also has a temporary 'wraith' form which makes him invincible for a maximum of 3 seconds, or until cancelled/ reverted. Lastly, every 10 seconds he can teleport up to 35 in-game meters away, and he recovers health from dealing damage

Supports

Support heroes have a very self-explanatory job, which is to support and heal their teammates. There are 2 support heroes per team, and Unlike their tank and damage counterparts, support heroes have a vast range of abilities to heal the team, and boost their damage to different extents

I play the following 3 support heroes:

Moira

Moira deals the most damage out of the support heroes. She fires healing dust out of her left hand to heal her teammates, and she drains the life out of enemies with her right hand (also healing her slightly). She can also create either a healing or damage orb every 8 seconds and can temoorarily 'fade' away. This increases her mobility and removes any debuffs she may have

Kiriko

Kiriko is one of the best support heroes for all-round performance. She has 15 throwing knives and heals her team with a stream of slow-moving feathers. She can teleport to her teammates every 7 seconds and up to 35 in-game meters away, even through surfaces. Furthermore, she has a 'suzu' grenade which cleanses any teammate that's within its splash radius, and grants them immortality for 1 second

Ana

Ana has some of the most powerful healing and damaging abilities. She carries a sniper which either damages enemies or heals teammates. She also haS a biotic grenade which (if in the splash radius) slightly damages enemies and temporarily prevents them from being healed, and slightly heals teammates while making them easier to heal. Her last weapon is her sleep dart, which sleeps enemies for up to 5 seconds
